SHAIRI

safal hona chahte ho toh fal ki nhi
karm ki chinta karni chahiye

na sangharsh na taklif
toh kya maza hai jeene mein
bade bade toofan tham jate hai
jab aag lagi hai seene mein

doston se bichharh ke ye aihsas hua hai
beshak kamine the lekin raunak unhi se thi

kon kehta hai ki ishwar nazar nhi aata
sirf vahi toh nazar aata hai
jab koi nazar nhi aata

chawal agar kumkum ke sath mil jaye
toh kisike mastak tak pahuch jate hai
aur moong ke sath mile khichdi mein kaam aa jate hai
matlab aap kon ho uska mahatva nhi hai aap kiske saath ho uska mahatva hai

shairi by rekha jain
